Beach volleyball coaching - Level 1

Read the Beach

Simple skills, sharp decisions, in real conditions. A science-based coaching system built for the two-player game - wind, sun, sand and all.

TENET 01

The game teaches the game. Learn beach by playing beach - small-sided, decision-rich, not dead-ball reps.

TENET 02

Coach the effect, design the environment. Few cues, smart constraints. Set the problem; the player finds the answer.

TENET 03

Two players, every contact matters. Simplicity and adaptability over complexity. Read, decide, adapt.
